Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

RateThatBeach ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RateThatBeach mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Anonymous Identifier

When you first launch the App, Firebase Authentication assigns you a randomly generated anonymous user ID. This ID is not linked to your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

Ratings You Submit

When you rate a beach, we store your anonymous user ID alongside the beach identifier and the numeric rating (1–5). We also record a timestamp and a daily rating counter (to enforce the 20-rating-per-day limit).

Map Usage Data

Geographic coordinates of the map viewport are sent to Google Places API to discover beaches in the area. We do not log or store these coordinates on our servers.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data subject to Google's own privacy policy. We request non-personalized ads by default.

Crash and Performance Data

We may collect anonymous crash reports and performance metrics via Firebase to improve stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App.
  • Calculate and display average beach ratings.
  • Enforce the daily rating limit per user.
  • Show relevant nearby beaches on the map.
  • Display advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • Monitor and improve App performance and stability.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share anonymized data with:

  • Google / Firebase — for database hosting and crash reporting.
  • Google AdMob — for serving advertisements.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Maps SDK & Places API: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your anonymous data. You can initiate this directly from the App settings under the "Delete my data" section.
